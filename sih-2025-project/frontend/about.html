About Us

The Swasthya Health Card is a government-supported initiative to make healthcare affordable and accessible to all. With this card, individuals and families can access subsidized medical services, diagnostic tests, and medicines.

Our mission is to create a healthier society by ensuring that financial constraints never stop someone from receiving proper healthcare.
